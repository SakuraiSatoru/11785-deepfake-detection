To download the FaceForensics++ dataset please use the download script available here and consult our github for usage instructions. The original FaceForensics script can be downloaded here and you will find additional information on its github.

This download script also includes the Deep Fake Detection Dataset which includes various high quality scenes of multiple actors which have been manipulated using DeepFakes.

We are also offering a benchmark on facial manipulation detection under compression which might be interesting to you under this link. You can download the benchmark images here.
